The Jazz Lanterns trio started over a shared love of smooth jazz classics; the likes of Gershwin, Ella Fitzgerald, Billie Holiday and Nina Simone.

It wasn't long before we started crafting out own soulful jazz reinventions of recent pop, rock and dance hits.

We are available for functions, hotel lounge, bar and restaurant sets, and much more. Our set list is flexible and we can help you create the perfect sound and style for your event.



Who are we?

ROSIE CARR

London College of Music educated singer with deep, smooth vocals suited to jazz and soul

STEPHEN THEOFANOUS
